What is STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Mathematics)


STEAM is an approach to education that empowers students to be curious learners who seek creative solutions to real-world problems. STEAM education helps students develop the capacity to:

• Become resilient problem solvers

• Engage in meaningful learning activities

• Work through the creative process

• Embrace and appreciate collaboration

• Take thoughtful risks

• Develop the soft and hard skills necessary to succeed

Why Choose Us?

Education choices should not be limited to school near you or stopped by pandemics.

Qualified Staff Members

We believe in the importance of each child receiving plentiful attention, hence each staff member undergoes a reference, character and credential check prior to commencing employment


Open Communication

We work with parents to promote open communication for the mutual benefit of the child. We welcome parents to speak with teachers via e-mail or phone. Weekly child activity updates will be sent to parents via emails




Security

Online lessons are recorded to provide a form of security and general control over the operations at the school. This also allows pupils and parents to revisit the lesson at any time, ensuring that no topic or subject is left without being fully understood.



Specialty Programs

Although the school will be using Cambridge and ECZ programs, we will also be offering specialty subjects and extra-curricular activities to give our children an enriched education experience.
