About  Us


We aspire to accelerate human progress through the design of meaningful online learning

Olla Academy was created to provide a comprehensive education program that teaches kids more than theory but how to apply what has been taught to real life situations.

The school is designed to provide great education that comes to you and follows you were ever you go. We welcome learners from across Zambia and Internationally.

Our Mission is to provide an enriching environment for each child to develop life-long skills of independence
